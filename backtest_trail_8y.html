📖 Strategy Justification — Why Trail Analyzer Works

  • Trend Following: Only picks stocks in confirmed uptrend (Price > MA20 > MA50). Avoids catching falling knives.
  • Dynamic Stop Loss: Chandelier Exit (Highest High − 3.0× ATR) adapts to each stock's volatility. High-vol stocks get wider stops, low-vol stocks get tighter stops.
  • Risk% Ranking: Picks stocks with the most room above their trailing stop = best risk/reward entry point in the trend.
  • Monthly Rotation: Re-evaluates every month. Losers get replaced, winners may be held if still in uptrend.
  • Buy on Open, Sell on Close: Realistic execution — no lookahead bias. Entry at month start, exit at month end.

⚠️ Risks & Limitations

  • Survivorship Bias: Only tests current HSI blue chips. Delisted stocks are excluded.
  • No Transaction Costs: Results don't include brokerage fees, slippage, or stamp duty (~0.1% per trade in HK).
  • Concentration Risk: Only holds 2 stocks per month. A single bad pick can significantly impact monthly returns.
  • Momentum Whipsaw: In choppy markets, trend signals can flip frequently, generating losses.
  • Past Performance: Historical returns don't guarantee future results.
